## The Role of Personal Trainers

Personal trainers have long been the go-to for those seeking customized workout plans, motivation, and accountability. They bring a wealth of experience and a personal touch that can make all the difference in a client’s fitness journey. Trainers can assess your form, provide immediate feedback, and tailor sessions on the fly based on your energy levels and progress.

However, while the personal touch is invaluable, it comes with limitations. Trainers are often booked with clients, which can make scheduling a hassle. Moreover, each trainer has their own philosophy and methods, which can vary widely. This is where AI can step in to complement and enhance the fitness experience.
